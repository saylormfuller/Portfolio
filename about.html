A little about me

I'm a UX/UI designer who combines user-centered design with strategic business thinking to create products that people love to use. I specialize in designing end-to-end experiences, translating research insights into intuitive interfaces, scalable systems, and impactful product features.

I graduated from the BYU Marriott School of Business with a degree in Experience Design and Management. I've worked across both global organizations and early-stage startups, partnering with cross-functional teams to design solutions that balance user needs with business goals.

From discovery and research to prototyping and launch, I've led the design of new features from concept through implementation, helping teams solve complex problems and deliver meaningful value to both users and the business.
