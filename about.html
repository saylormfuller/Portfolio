A little about me

Hi there, it's nice to meet you! I'm Saylor, a designer of experiences. I love to create products that people love.

As a UX generalist, I do a bit of everything, from discovery and research to prototyping and launch. I design end-to-end experiences, collect and translate research insights into intuitive interfaces, scalable design systems, and impactful product features.

I graduated from the BYU Marriott School of Business with a degree in Experience Design and Management, giving me an edge in the design world. Not only do I specialize in design solutions that address user needs, but I also have an in-depth understanding of how to tackle business goals. I've worked with both global organizations and early-stage startups.
